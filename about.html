About

A decade of shipping AI that works.

Alexander Kwok is a Head of Data Science & ML Engineering based in Sydney, Australia. Over the past decade, he has built and led teams that ship production AI/ML systems — from petabyte-scale data platforms at AWS to personalised recommendation engines serving millions of customers at Woolworths, to building an entire data organisation from scratch at Linkby.

Head of Data Science & ML Engineering
