# LinkedIn Post — AI Landscape Knowledge Graph

Copy and paste this into LinkedIn. Pair it with a screenshot or screen recording of the interactive graph.

---

I mapped the entire AI ecosystem into one interactive graph.

80+ nodes. 100+ connections. 6 categories.
Models, companies, researchers, concepts, papers, and infrastructure — all rendered as a force-directed knowledge graph you can explore in your browser.

Three things jumped out that you don't see from reading the news:

1. Everything traces back to one paper.
"Attention Is All You Need" (2017) is the most connected node in the graph. GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, LLaMA — every frontier model is architecturally downstream of 8 researchers at Google Brain.

2. The real moat is infrastructure, not models.
NVIDIA + CUDA have more connections than any single model. Hugging Face connects to more open-source projects than most companies have employees. The picks-and-shovels thesis isn't a cliché — it's visible in the topology.

3. Open-source is now as dense as closed.
Meta AI → LLaMA → PyTorch → Hugging Face → Mistral forms a self-reinforcing cluster that rivals the OpenAI–Microsoft graph. DeepSeek sits at the intersection of both.

Built with: D3.js, vanilla JS, a single HTML file, and a lot of manual data curation. No frameworks. No backend.
